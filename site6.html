Avani Benthota Resort

Located on a peninsula all our own, a world of adventure waits for you at the newly renovated Avani Kalutara. On water: jet and water skiing. On land: rifle shooting and archery. And up high: rock climbing, abseiling and ziplining – there really is something for everyone, no matter your vibe.
In between, you’ll enjoy downtime on our private beach, poolside, or in the Anantara Spa at our sister resort adjacent. Island life doesn’t get better than this.
