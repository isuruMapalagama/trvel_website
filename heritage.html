Aluthgama

Aluthgama is a coastal town in Kalutara District in the Western Province of Sri Lanka.
It is approximately 63.5 kilometres south of Colombo and 68 kilometres north of Galle. Aluthgama is situated on the southern bank of the Bentota Ganga mouth. The main income is from tourism and banking.
